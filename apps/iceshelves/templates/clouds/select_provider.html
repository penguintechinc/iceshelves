Getting Started

To add a cloud provider:

  1. Click on your desired cloud provider above
  2. Follow the wizard to configure credentials and settings
  3. Test the connection before saving
  4. Start deploying eggs to your new cloud!

All credentials are encrypted and stored securely. You can choose to store them in the database, AWS Secrets Manager, GCP Secret Manager, or Infisical.
